Our Facilities

Four venues.
One address.

01 — Marriage Lawn

An open-air lawn built for grand weddings

Our marriage lawn is designed to comfortably host large-scale celebrations of 500 to 1000+ guests — with room for a mandap, seating, catering counters and evening decor.

02 — Banquet Hall

A spacious indoor hall for every occasion

For ceremonies, receptions, corporate events and gatherings that need a covered, air-conditioned setting — our banquet hall offers a flexible layout that adapts to your guest count and format.

03 — Restaurant

In-house dining, run by our own kitchen

Our on-site restaurant serves guests, families and event attendees alike — and doubles as our in-house catering for weddings and functions hosted on the lawn or in the hall.

04 — AC Rooms

Comfortable stay for outstation guests

Air-conditioned rooms on the property mean family and guests travelling from out of town have somewhere comfortable to stay, right where the celebration is happening.
