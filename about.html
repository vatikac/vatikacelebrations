Our Story

Built to host Seoni's grandest celebrations

Vatika Celebrations was built around a simple idea: a family hosting a wedding shouldn't have to run between five different vendors to get a venue, a caterer, a hall and a place for guests to stay. We own and operate our restaurant, marriage lawn, banquet hall and AC rooms ourselves — all on one property in Lugharwara, Seoni.

That means one point of contact, one team that knows every corner of the property, and a celebration that's planned as a whole rather than stitched together from separate bookings.
